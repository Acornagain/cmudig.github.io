Titus Brown - Dominik Moritz - Michael O'Brien - Felix Reidl - Taylor Reiter - Blair Sullivan blog: http://ivory.idyll.org/blog/2018-spacegraphcats-paper.html doi: 10.1101/462788 link: https://www.biorxiv.org/content/early/2018/11/05/462788 pdf: https://www.biorxiv.org/content/early/2018/11/05/462788.full.pdf short_doi: 10/cwrw tags: - Science - Genomics title: Exploring neighborhoods in large metagenome assembly graphs reveals hidden sequence diversity type: - Preprint venue: bioRxiv preprint venue_tags: - bioRxiv year: 2018 teaser: assets/publications/spacegraphcats.jpg --- Genomes computationally inferred from large metagenomic data sets are often incomplete and may be missing functionally important content and strain variation. We introduce an information retrieval system for large metagenomic data sets that exploits the sparsity of DNA assembly graphs to efficiently extract subgraphs surrounding an inferred genome. We apply this system to recover missing content from genome bins and show that substantial genomic se- quence variation is present in a real metagenome. Our software implementation is available at https://github.com/spacegraphcats/ spacegraphcats under the 3-Clause BSD License.
